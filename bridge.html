Bridge Loans

Short-term capital that gives you time and flexibility between acquisitions, dispositions, or refinances.

Overview

Bridge to Your Next Move

Bridge loans provide short-term financing when you need to move fast or need time between transactions. Whether you're acquiring a property before selling another, stabilizing an asset before placing permanent debt, or simply need capital to close on a time-sensitive deal, bridge financing gets it done.

Our bridge products are built for speed. We underwrite the deal, not just the borrower, and structure terms that match your exit strategy.

Loan Range

$500,000 to $3,000,000

Term

6 to 24 months

Closing Speed

As fast as 5 to 10 business days

Exit Strategy

Sale, refinance, or permanent debt placement

Why Ledger

What Sets Us Apart

  • Close in as little as one week for qualified borrowers
  • Flexible terms that match your specific exit strategy
  • No prepayment penalties on most products
  • Interest-only payments during the loan term
  • Extension options available if your timeline shifts

Common Use Cases

Acquiring a property before selling an existing asset. Stabilizing a recently renovated or newly constructed property before placing permanent financing. Closing on a time-sensitive deal when traditional lending can't move fast enough.

Bridge loans are the most flexible tool in a real estate investor's toolkit. If you have a clear exit and need speed, this is the product.
