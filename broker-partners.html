For Brokers

Your Clients Build. We Fund. You Earn.

Ledger Trade & Capital gives brokers and loan originators direct access to a full suite of residential investment loan products, from ground-up construction to stabilized rental financing. No retail channel competition. No runaround.

We built this program because we know the best deals come through brokers who are close to their markets. You bring the borrower and the deal. We bring fast underwriting, reliable capital, and programs that actually close.

Products Available

Full Product Suite, Wholesale Rates

  • Ground-Up Construction — single-family, townhomes, small multifamily
  • Bridge — acquisitions, value-add, repositioning
  • Fix & Flip — rehab financing with draw schedules
  • DSCR Rental — long-term hold, no personal income docs
  • Loans up to $20M — nationwide, institutional capital
