How we compared

  • One source only. Every competitor number is pulled from their own website (product pages, guidelines, rate sheets, FAQs). No BiggerPockets, no broker chatter, no third-party scorecards.
  • Three products. Each page covers ground-up construction, DSCR rental, and fix-and-flip where that lender publishes them. Missing products are called out, not guessed.
  • “Not published” is a real answer. If a lender doesn’t disclose a parameter publicly, we say so rather than invent a number.
  • Dated. Every table is tagged “Verified April 2026.” We re-verify quarterly; pricing and guidelines move.
  • No rankings. Lenders appear in alphabetical order. There’s no “best of” list, because the right answer depends on your deal.
